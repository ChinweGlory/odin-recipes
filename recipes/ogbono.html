Ogbono-Okro Soup

ogbono-okra-soup

This Nigerian soup is very delicous and can be eaten with any kind of fufu from semo to garri and even pounded yam

Ingredients

  • Ogbono
  • Okro
  • Smoked fish
  • Palm oil
  • Crayfish
  • Beef

Steps on how to prepare it

  1. Put beef in your pot and spicy it add water and start cooking
  2. After 15mintues add your smoked fish and stock fish
  3. In a plate add your ogbono then add palm oil to the ogbono and mix
  4. Add the ogbono
  5. Add sliced okro
