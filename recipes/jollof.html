Jollof Rice

Nigerian Jollof Rice

The popular jollof rice is a classic recipe that makes it to almost all special occasions in Nigeria. It is a rich, tasty, and elegant meal

Serve this amazing meal with any protein of your choice.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Spicies
  • Rice
  • Groundnut Oil

Steps on how to prepare Jollof Rice

  1. parboil your rice
  2. put groundnut oil in a pot and allow to heat up a bit
  3. Add you sliced Onions
  4. Add tomatoes and pepper
  5. Add meat stock
  6. Add parboiled rice
