Egg Sauce

Egg sauce is very easy to prepare and can be eaten with Rice, Yam and even Bread

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Eggs
  • spicy

Steps on how to prepare

  1. In a clean pot, put your groundnut oil
  2. Put sliced Onions
  3. Put your sliced or blended tomatoes
  4. Add your spicy
  5. Add the eggs
